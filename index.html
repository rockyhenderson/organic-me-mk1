For over two decades, Organic Me has been your trusted source for delicious, organic honey in the Scottish Borders. Founded in 1996, we've been dedicated to crafting nature's finest honey, straight from the heart of this picturesque region. Taste the essence of the Scottish countryside in every jar of our golden nectar.

Why choose us

Choose Organic Me for your honey and organic produce needs. As the oldest organic honey producer in Scotland, with a history dating back to 1996, we proudly serve over 10,000 loyal customers. When you choose Organic Me, you're choosing a trusted and time-tested source for both nature's golden nectar and the freshest organic delights.
