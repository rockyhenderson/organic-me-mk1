Our Mission

Ecommerce has transformative powers, and we believe everyone should be able to take part. But outdated solutions and overwhelmed systems create uncertainty and risk.


We empower businesses to realize the full potential of eCommerce by making it safe, accessible, and frictionless.


We’ve built an eCommerce risk management platform that allows online merchants to create trusted relationships with consumers. Leveraging machine learning, our platform aggregates data from many of the world’s largest online merchants and creates network effects that drive higher sales and generate cost savings for our merchants. By accurately identifying the individuals behind online interactions, our technology enables merchants to provide frictionless customer experiences.

ORGANICME HISTORY

1996

The Birth of OrganicMe

In 1996, organicME was founded in the heart of the enchanting Scottish Borders, a region renowned for its lush, rolling hills and pristine landscapes. The founders, inspired by the region's natural beauty, set out on a mission to provide the highest-quality organic produce to the people of Scotland.

1998

Our First Harvest

OrganicMe proudly celebrated its first successful honey harvest in the rich fields of the Scottish Borders. Our organic honey, lovingly produced by our expert beekeepers, quickly gained a reputation for its exceptional taste and purity.

2003

Expanding Horizons

Responding to the growing demand for organic foods, OrganicMe expanded its product range to include not only honey but also a diverse selection of organic vegetables and meats. This expansion allowed us to offer a wide array of organic options, catering to various dietary preferences.

2005

Commitment to Sustainability

In 2005, we made a firm commitment to sustainability. OrganicMe embraced sustainable farming practices, ensuring that our products were not only healthy but also environmentally friendly. We implemented eco-conscious initiatives to reduce our carbon footprint and protect the pristine Scottish environment we call home.

2010

OrganicMe Goes Online

Recognizing the importance of accessibility, OrganicMe launched its website in 2010. This online platform made it easier for people across Scotland to discover and order our high-quality organic produce. Our digital presence allowed us to reach a broader audience while maintaining our commitment to quality.

2015

Organic Food for All

In 2015, we forged partnerships with local communities and educational institutions to promote the benefits of organic food. OrganicMe was dedicated to not only providing organic produce but also educating consumers about the advantages of a healthy, organic lifestyle. Our community involvement initiatives empowered individuals to make informed choices about their food.

2020

Organic Excellence Recognized

OrganicMe's dedication to exceptional organic produce was rewarded when we received the prestigious "Best Organic Produce Supplier" award from the Scottish Organic Food Association in 2020. This recognition highlighted our unwavering commitment to quality and our ongoing efforts to support the organic food movement in Scotland.

2021

Expanding Distribution

Recognizing the growing demand for organic products, OrganicMe extended its reach beyond Scotland. We began supplying our premium organic food to neighboring regions, expanding our influence and sharing the goodness of organic living with a broader audience.

2023

A Sustainable Future

In 2023, OrganicMe continues to lead the way in the organic food industry. We remain dedicated to promoting sustainable and eco-friendly practices in agriculture and food production. Our commitment to a sustainable future is a core value that guides our every decision, ensuring that the legacy of OrganicMe endures for generations to come.
